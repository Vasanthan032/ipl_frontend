Welcome to IPL Analysis Website!


About the Project:

Our Website shows multiple Analysis of the Batsmen, Bowlers and Teams participated in Indian Premier League. The analysis is based on the factual data of each matches that are conducted in IPL tournament between the years 2008 - 2020.

To view the reports made for each stream of the game, the users are requested to create an account in our website by providing required credentials. The provided data by the users are protected and are maintained with respect to our website’s privacy policy. After creating the account, the users can view the batsmen, bowlers and teams reports in its respective tabs displayed in the top right corner of the screen.
